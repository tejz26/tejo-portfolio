Profile

Cyber Security & IoT undergrad who ended up living in spreadsheets, dashboards, and demand curves.

I'm a business analyst who spent the last year inside TenderCuts' supply chain, the part of retail nobody notices until it breaks. The rest of my time goes into the systems around that decision: Power BI dashboards that give leadership one source of truth, and Python/SQL automations that took manual invoice matching, GRN reconciliation, and daily reporting off my team's plate entirely.
